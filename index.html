Hello, World!

Dec 27th, 2013

Hey, there!

I built this site out of love for both the Rochester area and the Rochester community. We live in one of the most unique cities in the country, and our amazing culture should be celebrated for what it is!

Right now, I’m building this site to be a bi-weekly, centralized, curated list of cool and unique events occurring in the Rochester area. If you or anyone you know would like to advertise for an upcoming event, feel free to contact me by email (dan “at” rochesterhappenings “dot” com) or using Twitter (@roc_happenings).

Together, we can build a phenomenal site for a phenomenal city. Thanks, and I hope to see you again soon :)
